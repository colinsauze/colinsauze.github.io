About Me

I currently work as a Senior Research Software Engineer for the National Oceanography Centre.

Previously I worked for many years at Aberystwyth University. First as a research associate working on biologically inspired power management strategies for autonomous systems in the Computer Science Department. Then as the Data Manager at the National Plant Phenomics Centre in the Institute of Biological, Environmental and Rural Sciences at Aberystwyth University. Most recently as a Research Software Engineer for the Supercomputing Wales project.
